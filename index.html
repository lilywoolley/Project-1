Todd Howard

The man who made Skyrim

List of Todd Howard's works

  • 1995 - Produced The Terminator: Future Shock
  • 1996 - Produced SkyNET and assisted with design in The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall
  • 1998 - Project lead on The Elder Scrolls Adventures: Redguard
  • 2002 - Project lead on The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind
  • 2003 - Executive producer on The Elder Scrolls III: Bloodmoon
  • 2004 - Executive producer on The Elder Scrolls Travels: Shadowkey
  • 2006 - Executive producer on The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion
  • 2007 - Executive producer on The Elder Scrolls IV: Shivering Isles
  • 2008 - Game director of Fallout 3
  • 2011 - Game director of The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim
  • 2015 - Executive producer on Fallout Shelter and game director of Fallout 4
  • 2018 - Executive producer on Fallout 76
